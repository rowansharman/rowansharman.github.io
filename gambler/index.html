Gambler 500

Personal Project

Since 2022

In January of 2022 I bought a $1000 1991 Ford Ranger to participate in events with a group caled the Gambler 500 .

Since then, I have gone through the truck from top to bottom, doing upgrades and learning a everything there is to know about it.

  • Explorer 8.8 rear axle swap, including regear and full rebuild, re-weld perches and shock mounts. Upgrade to stronger axle with disc brakes and limited slip differential.
  • 6" suspension lift. Design and fabricate drop brackets for front. Upgrade to lifted Chevy 63s (Craigslist) in rear, including hangers, shackles, and custom bushings.
  • ABS drain pipe snorkel into stock airbox/filter
  • Wire all lights to ambulance switch panel with relays
  • General overlanding and trash-collecting upgrades: ladder rack, tool box, seat swap, tool kit
  • General roadworthiness and maintenance items: wheel bearings, brakes, engine valvetrain work, etc.
